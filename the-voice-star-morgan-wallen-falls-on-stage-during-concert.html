Country star Morgan Wallen, who got his start on The Voice Season 6, was recently captured on video taking a pretty big tumble while performing a concert. The singer fell over while singing on a fog-covered stage.

Morgan Wallen Falls on Stage During Concert

TMZ shared a video of Wallen’s fall, sharing that it happened Thursday while he was performing at the KFC Yum! Center in Louisville, Kentucky, as part of his current One Night at a Time World Tour. Toward the end of the concert, Wallen was performing his song “Heartless.”

As seen in the video, the stage was covered in a thick layer of fog that made it hard to see the floor. As Wallen walks forward toward the crowd, he loses his balance and falls over, seeming to disappear for a moment before standing back up and continuing to sing.

TMZ reports that, in addition to the fog, people who attended the show say there were also technical issues with the lighting at the bottom of the stage. It’s possible that this also contributed to Wallen’s fall on Thursday.

Fan Dubs the Singer ‘Morgan Fallen’ After the Incident

Fans also shared video of Wallen’s fall on TikTok after the incident. One clip shows him crouching on the edge of the stage right after picking himself up and making a funny face to the crowd as he continues to sing.

One fan definitely found humor in the situation, giving Wallen a new nickname. TikTok user @chunkeymonkey40 posted a video joking, “After this show, his name ain’t Morgan Wallen anymore. It’s Morgan Fallen.”

Wallen is scheduled to continue his One Night at a Time World Tour through the rest of the year, with more concert dates lined up around the United States and Canada over the next several months before he heads to London in December.

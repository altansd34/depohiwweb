20 People Whose Houses Are Really Out of the Ordinary

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  380 words  |  Tandra Barner

Houses have a lot of secrets. Very often, people can find artifacts and unusual details. Sometimes, there are entire secret rooms that even Harry Potter wouldn’t dream of!

“Found in an old house. Tops look like salt and pepper shakers”

“We tore up some carpeting in our house expecting concrete but instead found this ’70s masterpiece.”

“In 1965, my dad worked for his cousin, demolishing an old house. Inside one of the walls, he found a case with a violin.”

“The cousin told him he could keep it, so my dad had it for 50 years.”

“$1,700 in brand-new bills. This was the floor cut out for the safe in the back of a closet.”

“I took a wall out of my closet and found a makeshift passageway with a large heavy creepy basket in it.”

“Someone buried an old TV in our backyard at some point.”

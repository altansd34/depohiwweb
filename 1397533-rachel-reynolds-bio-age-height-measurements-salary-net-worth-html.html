Rachel Reynolds biography

This talented woman was born in Mandeville, Louisiana, United States. She went to Louisiana State University, where she studied Public Relations, and had a fruitful athletic career there, since she was on the university's track-and-field team.

During her studies, she also worked as a cashier to make some extra money. This was how she got recognized by an agent and convinced to pursue modeling, to which she quickly agreed.

In 2002, she took part in a competition for who would replace departed hostess Brooke Burke on the E! travel series Wild On! Although she was one of the most memorable competitors and reached the final, she ended up losing to Cindy Taylor.

However, her next venture became life-changing: she auditioned for the game show The Price is Right to become one of Barker's Beauties. The first episode that included her aired on June 6, 2003, and she currently is the fourth longest-serving model on the show, having appeared in multiple seasons throughout the years.

Rachel Reynolds car crash

Kentucky cowboy Alex Miller stole our hearts during his time on Season 19 of American Idol. His classic country sound and huge heart left the judges wowed and had fans falling in love. Did Alex or any of his fellow Season 19 contestants find love on the show?

Alex Miller Gives Update on His Love Life After ‘American Idol’

Alex went on his own journey to find love during his time on American Idol. In a clip that will pull at your heartstrings, Alex gifted fellow contestant Cameron McGhar a belt buckle. He was gutted to find out that she had a boyfriend, but his journey to find love is far from over even after his Hollywood Week elimination.

We put Alex in the hot seen during an exclusive interview to ask him if he found love on the show with another contestant or when he got home. He joined us from Alyssa Wray’s aunt’s house after watching the Perryville teen perform during the Disney Night episode.

“The contestant thing, I don’t know if there’s any love there, no,” he admitted. “But I’m definitely finding some love when I got home. My county and all of the people around us have been really supporting us and I can’t even go out and eat anymore.”

Alex is already back out into the dating world in the midst of his performances and appearances all over town.

“I went out with a couple of girls the other night,” he said. “We went out to eat. I couldn’t talk to girls with people coming over talking to me.”

Is Anyone From This Season Dating Each Other?

Will we be seeing another budding romance come out of American Idol this season? Gabby Barrett and Cade Foehner who met on Season 16 are now married and parents to a baby girl named Baylah May. They set the precedent for blossoming relationships to come out of the show.

“I don’t think you can have as cute of a couple as them two,” Alex said about the couple. “It would be really tough to top them. As far as I know, everybody on the show is as single as me.”

What Alex didn’t know is that behind-the-scenes, eliminated contestants Anilee List and Andrea Valles struck up a great relationship. Anilee posted a picture of the couple embracing on the beach. The comments section flooded with love from past and present American Idol contestants.

“Woe woe woe another American Idol love storrraayyyy!!” Season 16 winner Maddie Poppe wrote.

Wendy Williams was joined by entertainment journalist Devyn Simone for an update on rapper Tyga’s current legal battle. The host of The Wendy Williams Show commented that Tyga’s baby mama Blac Chyna reached out to her for help because she has nowhere to live.

Wendy Williams Exposed Blac Chyna On-Air For Asking For A Place To Live

Tyga is being sued by a landlord for unpaid rent. After renting a house for a year and moving out in April, the landlord is suing him for $200,000. This sum of money comes from back rent and damage to the floors, walls, countertops, and some very expensive wallpaper. Tyga posted videos on Instagram to try and say that the claims were false and that there is no visible damage to the property. The pair also spoke about how Tyga has a rap sheet of unpaid bills in the past. This led to the shocking revelations Wendy made about his baby mama Blac Chyna.

“He has a child with Black Chyna, right?’ the ‘Masked Singer’ star asked. “I told you Blac Chyna texts me regularly ‘Can you help me find a condo I have no place to live'” she blurted out. “Maybe she sent a blast to everyone she knows because it didn’t say, like ‘Dear Wendy.'” Wendy also said that she did not answer the text message because she does not want to be involved in any of the drama.

Tyga and Chyna share an eight-year-old son King Cairo. The pair first got together after meeting on the set of his “Rack City” music video in 2011. The demise of their relationship came when Tyga met Kylie Jenner and moved on to pursue a relationship with her. Chyna also has a two-year-old daughter Dream with ex Rob Kardashian. She previously filed a lawsuit against the Kardashians for defamation after their show Rob & Chyna was canceled.

Chyna Responded To The Claims On Instagram

In response to Wendy’s claims about the star needing a place to live, Chyna hit back. She posted a video on her Instagram story showing off all of her expensive cars. In another video, she flexed the view from the balcony of her house. There is also a huge pool down below. Looking at these videos, it seems like Chyna has a place to live. She has multiple streams of income along with promotional posts.

She currently racks in money from posting exclusive content on her OnlyFans account. She recently promoted the website on her Instagram account with a series of topless pictures. Chyna also has her own makeup line called Lashed Cosmetics along with a clothing line and fitness and cooking accounts as well. This led her to post a video of a bunch of orders coming in for her makeup line.

American Idol alum Emyrson Flora has recently given her social media followers a treat. She recently released a new single titled “For Keeps.” The singer also teased that she’s been recording more songs.

Emyrson Flora Drops New Song “For Keeps”

In one of her most recent posts on Instagram, Flora announced that she’s already released the full version of “For Keeps.” The American Idol alum posted a snippet of the song alongside a video of the same.

“Full song on youtube now,” she wrote. “P.s. recording some cool stuff this week.”

On July 14, Flora revealed that she’s been working on a single, considered to be a “new song idea.” At the time, the singer uploaded a glimpse of her creative process on YouTube with a raw video of her casually singing a significant part of the song.

Within the video, Flora was seen wearing a statement T-shirt and a printed skirt while belting the lyrics of the song, as she plays her guitar. In the post, she asked her fans if she should release a studio version of the single.

“Heading into the studio this week eeeek!!! Should I record this one??” she wrote.

Ahead of this release, Flora has also dropped another original song on her YouTube channel. Titled “Build Yourself Up,” the song is expected by her fans to be included in her future music collection.

Fans Are Loving This New Song

Upon hearing “For Keeps,” Flora’s followers immediately praised her vocals. Several fans have expressed how proud they are of Flora for releasing new music.

“Man I have been writing for 2 years and am a 17 year old artist myself and this song is so so good,” one person wrote.

“SO SO AWESOME EMMY!! killer song, so proud,” another follower wrote.

“Love the voice, unique,” one person wrote.

While Flora has stated that she’s recording some cool stuff this week, she hasn’t given any details about the same yet. Be that as it may, many fans are thrilled that Flora has more in store for them.

Flora was the youngest contestant to compete in American Idol season 20. During the show, she revealed that it was her first real attempt to perform live before a big crowd. After her stint on American Idol, she’s been navigating her music career.

How to check MTN number in 2024

We are happy to list the three most popular methods for checking your MTN number. You can easily choose the one you like the most or find the easiest in our selection.

Method 1: Code to check MTN number

Like other Nigerian companies, MTN ensures its clients always get quick access to their personal details and information. You can use the USSD code and learn what number you have within seconds.

  • You need to dial one USSD code and perform some further actions. *123# - this is the quickest combination to dial directly on your device to see your personal MTN phone number on the screen. You will access the Menu.
  • Now you are welcome to choose option 1. Once you make this choice, you will get to the My Tools feature.
  • Click another option 1, known as ‘My Number.’ This is how each MTN client can request his or her phone number.
  • You will now see your phone number.

    Here is the full USSD you will have to dial and a second option to use:

    • *123*1*1# - your MTN number will be shown on your screen
    • *663# - you will see the phone number on the display

    Method 2: MTN self-service

    MTN has a self-service portal, which is available 24/7. However, it requires your phone number and PIN to log in.

    There is also a special self-service mobile application you can download and install directly on your smartphone. Here is how it works:

  • When you have connected to the MTN network, you can text a short message ‘Care’ to a quick number 5018.
  • You will get a responding SMS with a link that leads to the application download. It is called MyMTN App.
  • Install the program and use it to check all kinds of information about your account.

    • Method 3: Request call back

    The mobile operator offers a Call Me Back feature. You can text such a message to your friend and see your handset number. Use the quick combination *133# to perform this action. This feature is free of charge.

    Method 4: Call your friends

    This is a good method if you have airtime on your account. Just call anyone from your contact list and ask to name your phone number, which is always displayed when a person is calling.

    Method 5: Use customer service

    You can also visit MTN’s site and find their Customer Care phone number. Call it and ask what phone number you have.

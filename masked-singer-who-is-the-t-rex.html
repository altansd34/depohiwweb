The Masked Singer introduced its last set of Season 3 celebrities in Group C. And from the second she took the stage, T-Rex was full of energy. Plus, she has some dancing skills for sure! But who is it?

Here are the clues and predictions for the T-Rex so far!

Clues On The Masked Singer Season 3

In her clues, the T-Rex sits on a baby doll, then a directional sign with “N” and “W” circled is shown. She says she was discovered by another who “plucked her out of obscurity,” as a man pulls a dinosaur toy out of pink slime. We see a bow while T-Rex explains an extraordinary placed called “T-Rex Island.” She became one of dozens of creatures “pirouetting the land” there. Two T-Rexes are seen trying to fight inside bubbles. Then, a golden “U” with pink, blue, and white stars on it is displayed. But then T-Rex mentions she heard that unless you were special, “you were doomed.” Next, something happened that changed her world forever. That’s when we see “TO BE CONTINUED” on the screen.

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer T-Rex

Ken guessed the T-Rex was Kourtney Kardashian, Jenny thought it might be Maddie Ziegler from Dance Moms, and Nicole predicted Rebecca Black. She did appear on The Four not long ago, after all! But online, the predictions were much different. Most people think it’s JoJo Siwa but other popular guesses include Liza Koshy and Maddie Ziegler.

Our Prediction For The T-Rex

The panelists may be a little too old for this celebrity but the The Masked Singer T-Rex’s identity is obvious. It is…JoJo Siwa!

Watch the dance moves, listen to her voice, and this one is easy to figure out.

The beginning clue with the baby and directional map are alluding to JoJo Siwa babysitting North West. We then see pink slime, which JoJo has famously made on her YouTube channel. T-Rex says she was “discovered by another” and JoJo rose to fame after being discovered by Abby Lee Miller on Abby’s Ultimate Dance Competition. “T-Rex Island” and the “dozens of creatures pirouetting the land” are a nod to Dance Moms, which JoJo later appeared on. The men in black in bubbles is even an allusion to a popular dance from the show, where JoJo danced in a bubble.

Also in the clues, we see a bow (like a bow and arrow), but it is a nod to the bows JoJo usually wears in her hair. The gold “U” can be interpreted one of two ways. It’s either a reference to YouTube or her song “I Can Make U Dance” that uses the letter. At the end, when talking about “extinction,” it could be talking about how she was eliminated in 5th place on Abby’s Ultimate Dance Competition. So what’s the “TO BE CONTINUED”? Well the next clue package should talk about her illustrious time on Dance Moms and YouTube career!

More Clues Next Week

Did we miss any clues for The Masked Singer T-Rex? There will be plenty more when Group C returns for the Playoffs on Wednesday at 8/7c on FOX.

17 Clumsy Pet Moments That Were Luckily Caught on Camera

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  288 words  |  Billy Koelling

Each of us has probably seen little puppies go crazy playing with a new ’toy’ they found. Even if this toy is one of your favorite boots. Or noticed a pets’ genuine interest in your new electronic device with their irrepressible desire to chew every wire. Hopefully, many owners have mastered the art of catching their pets on camera in the most hilariously awkward situations. And they keep sharing all their joy and amusement with everyone around the globe.

We at Bright Side gathered some of these fabulous shots, which will surely make anyone smile and have a good laugh.

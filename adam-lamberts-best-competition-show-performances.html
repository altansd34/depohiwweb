Powerhouse vocalist and electrifying performer, Adam Lambert rose to fame on American Idol, yet he continues to take the stage as a guest at various other talent shows, showcasing his undeniable talent and charisma. Let’s take a trip down memory lane and revisit Lambert’s most unforgettable competition show performances that solidified his status as one of the greatest vocalists of his generation.

Adam Lambert’s Best Competition Show Performances

8. “Whole Lotta Love” – American Idol Season 8

Lambert was the first American Idol contestant ever to be given permission to cover a Led Zeppelin song. He took full advantage of this opportunity with his performance of “Whole Lotta Love” for one of his Top Four performances. Lambert’s energy and charisma in singing this song were undeniable.

7. “Somebody To Love” – X Factor Series 11

During The X Factor UK‘s series 11, Lambert once again joined forces with the iconic rock band Queen for a truly unforgettable performance of “Somebody To Love.” This time, he took the stage with the band as a guest performer, and their collaboration was nothing short of spectacular.

6. “Cryin'” – American Idol Season 8

During his unforgettable run on American Idol‘s eighth season, Lambert showcased his versatility by taking on Aerosmith’s classic rock hit “Cryin’.” With his signature vocal range and distinctive style, Lambert put his own spin on the song, infusing it with emotion and energy.

5. “Chandelier” – America’s Got Talent: All-Stars

Lambert performed “Chandelier” by Sia together with Aidan Bryant and Bello Sisters at the 2023 AGT: All-Stars finale. The performance was a perfect blend of talent and artistry. Lambert’s vocals were on point, and the dancers were incredible. This performance was a true showstopper.

4. “Bohemian Rhapsody” – American Idol Season 8

Lambert’s journey on American Idol began with a striking audition that left a lasting impression on the judges. His rendition of “Bohemian Rhapsody” showcased his extraordinary vocal range and unique style. This audition foreshadowed the remarkable performances that would follow, and also set the stage for his impressive run on the show.

3. “Mad World” – American Idol Season 8

In a defining moment during his time on American Idol‘s eighth season, Lambert delivered a spine-chilling rendition of “Mad World” by Tears for Fears. His unique interpretation of the song remains one of the most memorable moments in Idol history, earning him widespread acclaim from both the judges and fans alike.

2. “Evil in the Night” – X Factor Australia Season 8

Not only known for his powerful vocals but also for his mesmerizing stage presence, Lambert showcased his unique artistry as a judge on X Factor Australia. During an exciting episode, Lambert brought electrifying Halloween vibes with his performance of “Evil in the Night.” His dynamic performance combined with both haunting visuals, and his signature vocals, create a spine-tingling spectacle.

1. “I Can’t Stand the Rain” – American Idol Season 21

Returning to the Idol stage on Season 21’s Top 12 episode, Lambert showcased his timeless artistry with a powerful cover of Anne Peebles’ 1973 hit “I Can’t Stand the Rain.” Lambert’s electrifying rendition infused the R&B classic with his signature glam magic. His performance surrounded by digital purple rain showers was truly spectacular.

Pop sensation Mariah Carey is getting ready to spread even more cheer this 2023 holiday season. The 54-year-old singer and her 12-year-old twins, Moroccan and Monroe, star in a holiday campaign for The Children’s Place.

Mariah Carey Models Together With Twins In a New Holiday Campaign

It’s clear that Carey is no stranger when it comes to the holiday season. With a career filled with iconic Christmas music and a deep love for the holidays, her partnership with The Children’s Place feels like a perfect match.

“The Children’s Place is a household name, and it truly dominates the children’s apparel space, so I’ve always been a fan of theirs,” Carey said. “Secondly, I’m just someone who absolutely loves the holidays. I mean, that’s obvious, but I really do love holidays, specifically Christmas.”

For Carey, the holidays are a time for family and togetherness. She and her twins, Moroccan and Monroe, often sport matching pajama sets. The Children’s Place campaign, An Iconic Holiday: Part 3, showcases Carey and her twins in festive holiday attire, spreading the warmth and cheer of the season.

This campaign follows the footsteps of previous parts featuring celebrities like AJ McLean, Lance Bass, Joey Fatone, Wanya Morris, Snoop Dogg, and their families, underlining the brand’s commitment to celebrating the holidays in style.

Collaborating with her beloved twins, whom she affectionately calls “Dem Babies,” was a truly enjoyable experience for Carey. “We had a blast on set. They’re really such troopers” she shared.

Mariah Carey Designs a Dress For The Children’s Place

Aside from the delightful matching pajama sets, Carey’s partnership with The Children’s Place introduces a show-stopping piece: “The Dress.” Initially hesitant about taking on a designing role, Carey decided to create something classic and timeless.

She envisioned a stunning red ball gown with a voluminous skirt, reminiscent of her early days in the music industry. This gown embodies her unique style and love for all things elegant.

Carey’s involvement in crafting this special item was a highlight of the collaboration. “That was something that was really fun,” she said.

This dress is sure to add a touch of glamour to any holiday celebration. If you’re eager to get your hands on The Children’s Place’s latest collection, you’re in luck.

The holiday collection is now available online, in stores across the United States and Canada, and on the retailer’s Amazon storefront. You can now dress your little ones in style for the festive season, taking inspiration from Carey’s love for the holidays and her iconic sense of fashion.

America’s Got Talent: All-Stars is set to premiere on Monday, and we’ve already gotten a sneak peek at one of the performances we can look forward to seeing. The show shared an early release video showing the Bello Sisters performing an incredible balancing act.

Bello Sisters Perform in AGT: All-Stars Early Release

The Bello Sisters are an Italian-German trio who competed on AGT Season 15 with an amazing acrobatic act in which they balanced on each other’s hands and feet. They made it to the Finals, but they had to perform from Germany because of the Covid-19 pandemic.

Now they’re back on the Got Talent stage for another chance to win. They shared that they “have not stopped practicing” since they last appeared on the show, and teased that they would be “doing something that we haven’t done before” for their first AGT: All-Stars performance.

The sisters started off with the kind of gravity-defying moves we’ve come to expect from them, as they created towers out of their bodies and showed off their incredible strength and balance. Then they took the act new new heights as the platform they were on was raised.

What Did the Judges Think of the Performance?

“This is what All-Stars is all about!” host Terry Crews said to the camera as all three judges gave the Bello Sisters a standing ovation. “This is another level right now!”

“You girls have the kind of act that you just don’t want it to stop,” judge Heidi Klum told the sisters after their performance. “You have beauty and grace. It’s excitement, and I love you girls. And I’m so happy that you’re here.”

Howie Mandel marveled at how dangerous the act was, calling it “the epitome of women supporting women.” Meanwhile, Simon Cowell called the performance “about as close to perfection I’ve ever seen.” He added that if he was a Superfan, he would vote for them to move on.

Be sure to tune in to the premiere of AGT: All-Stars on Monday, January 2, at 8/7c on NBC.

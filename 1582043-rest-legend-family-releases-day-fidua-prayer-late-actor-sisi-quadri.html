Rest on Legend: Family Releases 8th Day Fidua Prayer for Late Actor Sisi Quadri

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  376 words  |  Martina Birk
  • The family of late actor, Tolani Quadri, aka, Sisi Quadri have released his eighth-day Fidua prayer plan
  • According to the flyer sighted on social media, it would take place on Saturday, March 9, 2024, in Iwo, Osun state
  • It was gathered that the late thespian was survived by his aged father, his children, and his siblings 

The eighth-day Fidau prayer for the repose of late thespian, Tolani Quadri, aka, Sisi Quadri, would take place on Saturday.

Legit.ng had reported that the actor died after an undisclosed ailment while he was in Iwo, Osun state.

In a flyer sighted by Legit.ng, the prayer would take place on March 9, 2024, in his hometown, Iwo, Osun state.

Sisi Quadri is survived by father, kids

In the flyer, it was noted that the late actor, who is known for his feminine roles in movies was survived by his aged father, his children, and his siblings.

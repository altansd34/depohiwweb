Single Dad Cristiano Ronaldo Found His Second Half in Georgina Rodrguez While Shopping, and It Was

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  358 words  |  Kelle Repass

Sometimes you’re lucky enough to meet that special someone to share your happy times and all the bumps in the road with, as life isn’t always a ride down easy street. And if you’re beyond fortunate, you get the opportunity to have your heart rates synchronized. Georgina Rodríguez and Cristiano Ronaldo had the chance to be that couple, given that they’ve been going strong for a long time now.

Bright Side is here to tell you the story of a couple that’s been through thick and thin and lived through it together, hand in hand.

“I have millions and billions, but the most important thing is family.”

One of the most essential things you should know about Cristiano Ronaldo is that he is a devoted dad. For him, loved ones always come first. Before meeting Georgina Rodríguez, he was already a father of 3. Little did he know, he’d soon meet a person to share his idea of family with.

The moment they met was unexpected, but they clicked instantly.

Georgina and Cristiano first met in 2016 when she was working as a shop assistant for Gucci. Outside the boutique, they passed each other, and it was a “click” moment, as Ronaldo explained. They decided to take things slow and started their relationship by talking as friends.

In 2017, they welcomed their first child together, Alana Martina. Georgina’s also been getting along great with Ronaldo’s first 3 kids, Cristiano Jr., Eva, and Mateo. In October 2021, they revealed happy news — they were expecting twins.

Support is a key word when it comes to life’s difficulties.

It seemed that everything was on track until Cristiano posted the heartbreaking news on his Instagram: “It is with our deepest sadness we have to announce that our baby boy has passed away. It is the greatest pain that any parent can feel.”

At that time, Ronaldo missed his game and stayed by his partner’s side to give her all the comfort she needed.

“When you are a father, it is a completely different feeling, a feeling that I cannot describe.”

It really looks like this couple hit the jackpot meeting each other — that is what they both say about the family concept: “Being a father is a unique and personal journey, something that has changed me completely,” said Ronaldo in an interview. “It’s taught me things about love that I never knew existed,” he added. “The children are our happiness — we wake up and the first thing we do is hug and kiss them,” says Rodríguez.

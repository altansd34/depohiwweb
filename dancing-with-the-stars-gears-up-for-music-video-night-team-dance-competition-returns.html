With eight remaining couples in the ballroom, Dancing With The Stars Season 32 gets tougher than ever. The upcoming episode will feature every dancing pair performing to the most iconic videos of all time. Fans must also brace for the contenders’ jaw-dropping battle of ages, the highly-anticipated Team Dance Competition.

Dancing With The Stars Team Dance Competition Comes Back

After a successful monster-themed Halloween episode, DWTS is now preparing to hit play for Music Video Night. The exciting night will also welcome famous singer-choreographer Paula Abdul as guest judge.

After by-pair dance routines, the eight pairs will split in half to compete in the team dance portion of the competition. Interestingly, the groupings this time fall into a compelling battle of ages: the four oldest celebrities versus the four youngest ones.

Team 4 Everybody will dance to “Everybody (Backstreet’s Back)” by Backstreet Boys. The group consists of film star Alyson Hannigan and pro Sasha Farber; GRAMMY-winning singer Jason Mraz and pro Daniella Karagach; Vanderpump Rules star Ariana Madix and Pasha Pashkov; and The Brady Bunch actor Barry Williams and pro Peta Murgatroyd.

Meanwhile, Team Young’n Style will perform to “Gangnam Style” by PSY. The team features reality TV star Harry Jowsey and pro Rylee Arnold; social media star Lele Pons and pro Brandon Armstrong; Marvel star Xochitl Gomez and pro Val Chmerkovskiy; and Former Bachelorette Charity Lawson and pro Artem Chigvintsev.

Every Song, Dance Style in Music Video Night Revealed

Alyson and Sasha are set to perform a Quickstep this week to “Candyman” by Christina Aguilera. Barry and Peta will perform in the same style, to “I’m Still Standing” by Elton John.

Meanwhile, four pairs will impress with a Jazz performance on Music Video Night. The first of the group is Xochitl and Val, who will be dancing to “Hollaback Girl” by Gwen Stefani. Meanwhile, Charity and Artem will perform in the same style, to “All For You” by Janet Jackson. Jason and Daniella will also be doing Jazz, to “Take On Me” by a-ha. The last pair to perform Jazz is Harry and Rylee, who will perform to “It’s Gonna Be Me” by *NSYNC.

Standout pair Ariana and Pasha will perform a Cha Cha to “I’m a Slave 4 U” by Britney Spears this week, while Lele and Brandon will perform a salsa to “Whenever, Wherever” by Shakira.

Dancing With The Stars Music Video Night premieres on Tuesday, November 7 at 8 p.m. ET. Fans can watch on both ABC and Disney+, with next-day streaming on Hulu.

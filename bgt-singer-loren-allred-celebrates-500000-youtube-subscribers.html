Britain’s Got Talent star Loren Allred recently hit 500,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel. To celebrate, the singer posted a video looking back at her journey over the years, all the way back to 2008.

Loren Allred Celebrates 500,000 YouTube Subscribers

The first clips in Allred’s post are from 2008 and 2009, as she sits at the piano with fellow musician Kris Kollins and sings. Next up is a clip from her time on The Voice in 2012, when she competed on Adam Levine’s team.

The post also features a clip of Allred’s collaboration with Michael Bublé on the song “Help Me Make It Through the Night” in 2018. After that, there’s a clip of her singing “Never Enough” from The Greatest Showman in 2019.

There’s also a clip from Allred’s 2021 music video for her song “Til I Found You.” That’s followed by moments from her time on Britain’s Got Talent earlier this year. The singer finished in ninth place during Series 15.

“Look at how far we have gotten!” Allred wrote about her latest YouTube milestone. “I am in awe and so grateful for every single one of you guys. My journey is all thanks to you. Excited to continue this adventure with you, and I can’t wait for all the memories to come!”

Allred Is Releasing a New Single Next Week

Allred recently teased a new single called “Aren’t You Tired” on social media. She shared a snippet of the song, in which she sings, “Aren’t you tired of running / Running away / Of all the hurting from your past mistakes.”

“Unpacking my emotional baggage in this next single,” Allred wrote on Instagram. “If you’ve ever dealt someone with who refuses to deal with their internalized problems, this song is for you.”

The new song will be released next Friday, December 9. Since competing on BGT earlier this year, Allred has released two other singles, including the original “Last Thing I’ll Ever Need” and a cover of Lauren Daigle’s “You Say.”

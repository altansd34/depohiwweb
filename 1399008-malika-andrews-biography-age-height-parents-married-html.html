Education

She studied at Head-Royce School until 8th grade when she was kicked out. Later she attended a year-round therapeutic boarding school in Utah, where she graduated at 17.

After high school, she worked at her grandfather's law firm for one year.

She joined the University of Portland for a Bachelor of Arts Degree. She graduated in 2017 with honours. At the University of Portland, she was the school's sports editor and the editor-in-chief of the university's student newspaper, The Beacon.

She won a National Association of Black Journalists scholarship and trained at the Sports Journalism Institute.

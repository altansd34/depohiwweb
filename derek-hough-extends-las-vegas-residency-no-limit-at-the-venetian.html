Derek Hough has announced the return of his Las Vegas show No Limit. The Dancing With The Stars alum posted on his social media that the show is coming back from May 18 through September 10. Tickets will be on sale this Friday. No Limit is held at the venetian resort and became the hottest ticket in town when it first launched in 2021.

“WE’RE BACK BABY! After last year’s incredible success we’ve decided to keep this Party going in LAS VEGAS !!! MAY- SEPTEMBER,” Hough was excited to share to his 3 million followers.

Fans were shocked when the announcement of Hough’s No Limit residency was made. He was a judge for Season 30 of Dancing With The Stars. Schedules worked out and the Emmy award winning dancer was able to perform both duties.

“I can’t wait to get back in there and put on an amazing show for you all. Here we go!!!” Hough wrote.

No Limit shows Hough as not only a dancer, but also a music artist who can sing and play the guitar. The 90-minute show features seven other dancers, including Hough’s longtime girlfriend Hayley Erbert. The two met during their time on Dancing With The Stars. The production has the cast performing everything from ballroom, salsa, and hip-hop.

Derek Hough’s Limitless Career

Hough has been one of the few dancers to use Dancing With The Stars as a launch pad towards a career in entertainment. Besides winning the mirror ball trophy six times, Hough has been nominated for 11 Emmys and has won three of the prestigious awards.

He became a best-selling author and joined another Vegas headliner, Jennifer Lopez, as a judge on their show World of Dance. He has also made appearances in High School Musical the series as well as Hairspray Live.

Although he is in-demand, Hough seems to enjoy returning to Dancing With The Stars. ABC has yet to announce Season 31 of the competition dance show. The Season 30 finale brought in close to 6 million viewers, so it is a sure bet an announcement will be happening soon.

Noah Thompson Teases New Song, Asks Fans If He Should Drop It

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  411 words  |  Billy Koelling

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing American Idol

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Country music star Noah Thompson is teasing new music on Instagram once again. The recently uploaded video shows Thompson wearing sunglasses and a black shirt, mouthing the lyrics to an unreleased song. Within the video, he asks fans if he should officially drop the song, or not.

Noah Thompson Showcases a New Original Song

On August 31, Thompson posed a question to his followers in a caption: “Should I drop this or nah?” After hearing a glimpse of the song in the video, fans got excited. Some have even been speculating about the theme and significance behind the upcoming track.

Since winning American Idol, Thompson has built a reputation for his soulful voice and relatable lyrics, and his followers are excited to see where this new musical journey will take him. The video has already garnered thousands of views, and fans are flooding the comments section with enthusiasm and anticipation.

In the same video, the American Idol Season 20 winner also asked fans if they could relate to the lyrics of the song, which seem to imply a failing relationship. Since so many can relate, fans are eager to hear the full song. They’re confident that it will be nothing short of a chart-topping hit.

While Thompson hasn’t unveiled an official release date for the song, he has hinted that it’s coming soon. In the meantime, fans should stay tuned to Thompson’s social media platforms for updates on the upcoming track.

Fans Noticed a Change In Noah Thompson’s Appearance

While the majority of Thompson’s comment section is talking about the song’s lyrics, some fans are talking about his look has changed. Majority of fans are fully on board with Thompson’s new look and sound, yet there are still some interesting reactions. Most of these reactions include a fire emoji, heart eyes, or even a heartfelt message.

Some fans are loving the longer hair, while others express nostalgia for his shorter style. Regardless, the consensus is clear: Thompson has his fans eagerly awaiting his next musical release, and they’re here to support him every step of the way.

In addition to the discussions about his music and appearance, Thompson’s fans also share their personal connections and fond memories of his journey. Some fans recall watching him grow as an artist from the beginning, while others appreciate the genuine and relatable aspects of his music.

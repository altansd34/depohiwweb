22 People Took a Stroll Down Memory Lane and Flawlessly Recreated Their Pics

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  351 words  |  Martina Birk

Ever since 1826, when the first photograph was taken, people have a way of traveling back in time and rekindling old memories. By looking at old photographs of ourselves or our loved ones, we get the chance to see how much we’re all connected to one another. And an even better way to see that is to put the old pic and the recreated pic side by side.

Recreating an imprint of a precious memory is an ongoing trend that we at Bright Side absolutely adore, and apparently many people are getting pretty good at making past memories come to life. We hope you enjoy 22 of the best-recreated pics that we’ve got lined up for you below.

1. “Not much has changed in 25 years.”

2. “Throwback Thursday, 1991 to 2021, back at the house I grew up in (Queens, NY)”

3. “Another 6 years later, and some things still don’t change.”

4. “Here are my parents on my mom’s twenty-first birthday (1988) and in the same spot 28 years later (2016).”

5. “My grandma and dad, 1966 and 2020”

6. “My father with me (1988) vs me with my daughter (2021)”

7. From a 10-year-old cub scout to a 27-year-old eagle scout — even the hat is the same! Go Braves!!!"

8. “15 years later and we still have that mall photo shoot swagger!”

9. “18 years and counting of being best friends, ages 5 and 23”

10. “My sisters and me, 20 years later, and 2 of them are expecting a baby.”

11. “My son and me vs my son and grandson”

12. “My husband and me in the same preschool class at 3 years old in 1994, and our kids on their first day of school in 2019”

13. “23 years later”

14. “My high school picture, freshman year, in 2004 next to my dad’s senior picture in 1975”

15. “Bomb pops, 1997 vs 2020”

16. “I posted a pic with my dad the other day but was told the resemblance didn’t come across, so here’s a better attempt.”

17. “Today’s my wedding anniversary. At the top is my wife and me in 2008, and at the bottom is our wedding day in 2018.”

18. “My family and me at Disneyland, CA, 13 years apart”

19. “Well, we tried...cousins. All were born about 3 months apart — there’s a 24-year difference here.”

20. “My sister got married over the weekend, so we recreated this gem from our childhood.”

21. “The family car and me, 20 years apart”

22. “My grandparents, April 5, 1961, and again on April 5, 2021”

Which of these recreated pics do you think really nailed it? Share your most impressive choices in the comments with us.

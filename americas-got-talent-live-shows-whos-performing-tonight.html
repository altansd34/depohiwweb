The AGT live shows are finally starting this week! Eleven acts will perform for the judges remotely, and America will vote overnight to decide who should move on. We’ll find out who made it in Wednesday’s results show.

Judge Simon Cowell suffered a back injury over the weekend, so he’ll be absent from Tuesday night’s show. But the exciting news is that Kelly Clarkson will be filling in for him as judge. Check out the night’s amazing line-up of acts below.

‘AGT’ Live Shows: This Week’s Acts

Bello & Annaliese Nock

This father and daughter daredevil duo previously competed on the show separately. They also performed together on BGT: The Champions. They auditioned virtually for this season of AGT with an amazing act involving a motorcycle and a tightrope. We can’t wait to see what they do for the live shows.

Archie Williams

This singer moved the judges with his story of being wrongly incarcerated for over 30 years and finally being exonerated. His emotional performance of Elton John’s “Don’t Let the Sun Go Down on Me” was an instant classic. What will he perform in the live shows? We’ll have to wait and see.

Brett Loudermilk

One of the funniest auditions of the season came from this sword swallower, who asked judge Sofia Vergara to help with a trick. Her hesitance was hilarious to watch. Brett even tried out a trick he’d never attempted before. It’s too bad Sofia can’t appear in person with him again.

Double Dragon

These twin sisters, who are originally from Peru, blew the judges away with their flashy outfits and their powerhouse duet of “It’s Raining Men.” They received four “si” votes for their audition. Here’s hoping they put out all the stops for the live shows.

RELATED: MEET ‘AGT’S DOUBLE DRAGON, THE TWIN SISTERS WITH POWERHOUSE VOICES

Feng E

This young musician from Taiwan plays the ukulele like you’ve never heard it before. For his audition, he played The Weeknd’s “Can’t Feel My Face” and received four “yes” votes from the judges. We can’t wait to hear his latest performance.

Frenchie Babyy

This street dancer has some shocking contortion moves. After sharing that his parents didn’t support his passion for dancing, Simon Cowell called Frenchie’s mom in front of the audience and shared that he was through to the next round.

Michael Yo

This former celebrity reporter didn’t let a lack of audience stop him from delivering a great stand-up comedy audition. The judges’ positive feedback brought him to tears. Since his audition, Michael has survived COVID-19, so it will be interesting to see if he talks about it in his latest routine.

Pork Chop Revue

We’ve seen plenty of dog acts on the show, but what about a group of trained pigs? Heidi Klum was so excited by the act, she just had to hop on stage and give one of the pigs a kiss. Unfortunately, she won’t be able to steal another smooch during tonight’s show, since the pigs will appear remotely.

Roberta Battaglia

This 10-year-old singer wowed the judges with her performance of Lady Gaga’s song “Shallow” from A Star Is Born. Sofia was so impressed, she hit the Golden Buzzer to send Roberta straight to the live shows. Tonight, she’ll deliver a brand-new performance.

Shaquira McGrath

Shaquira’s fabulous cover of “Redneck Woman” in the auditions impressed the judges, and she later returned for the Judge Cuts to sing “Wake Me Up” by Avicii. She made it through to the live shows, and we can’t wait to hear her latest performance.

Simon & Maria

These pint-sized Colombian salsa dancers delighted Sofia Vergara in their first audition. They returned for the Judge Cuts, and made it through to the next round. Tonight, they’ll dance for America’s votes.
